**Rain for Relax** is a minimalist and calming web app designed to help you relax, focus, or simply enjoy a peaceful moment. Whether you're winding down after a long day or need background ambiance to concentrate, this site delivers a seamless experience with curated chill music and the gentle sound of rain.

Just press play — sit back, close your eyes, and let the atmosphere guide you to tranquility.

### 🌟 Key Features

- A handpicked selection of soothing chill tracks
- Clean, distraction-free interface
- Continuous playback for uninterrupted relaxation
- High-quality audio for an immersive soundscape
- Fully open-source and accessible on any modern browser

### 🚀 How to Use

No setup required — just [visit the website](https://www.rain-for-relax.yuricunha.com) and start listening. Prefer running it locally? Clone the repo, open `index.html`, and you're all set.

### 🤝 Contribute Your Vibe

Want to share your own relaxing tracks or improve the app? Fork the project, add your music, and submit a pull request — it's that simple.
